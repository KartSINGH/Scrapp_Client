Welcome to Scrapp

 

 

We are here to help you manage your scrap and how to earn from it. We are into waste management and disposal which include all activities and actions required to manage waste from its formation. This includes from collection to receiving with proper monitoring and regulation.

 

You just need to call us or schedule pickup on our website and everything will get sort within 24 hours or less. Everything you think is a waste is not now anymore.
