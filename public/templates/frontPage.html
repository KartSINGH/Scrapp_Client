Schedule Waste Pickup

 

Post your requirements and schedule your pickup instantly!

You can put in your requirements via our helpline number or through our website. You can easily schedule a pickup in advance to conveniently dispose your waste whenever you are available at home.

Scrap Inspection

 

Our trained executives will reach your location for inspection and assess the correct value of your scrap material using ISO certified electronic weigh scales.

These executives are not your untrustworthy local Kabadiwale. They are employed by Shutlescrap and this helps us maintain high level of transparency.

Secure Delivery for Recycling

 

Shuttlescrap is powered by Deshwal Waste Management, Industry leader in e-waste,battery and plastic waste recycling. Deshwal has recycling plants in IMT Manesar,Haryana and Khushkhera,Rajasthan, where Shutlescrap directly delivers waste from your home.

Convert to cash or Transfer to e-wallet

 

Once we have calculated your scrap's worth, we pay you instantly in cash or transfer to your e-wallet.
